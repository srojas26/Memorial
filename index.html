ELAINE DOE

1981 - 2018

"Forever celebrating your life with joy"

  • Born on July 27th, 1981 in Havana, Cuba.
  • Transitioned on May 3rd, 2018 in Miami, FL, United States.

A beloved wife, and mother of 2 sons. Elaine lived in Havana, Cuba for the first 30 years of her life. It was there that she met her husband, Jose, and the couple started a family. Elaine had 2 older sisters, and 1 younger brother.

Elaine was a beautiful gentle soul, filled with humor, intellect, and optimism. She always had something inspirational to say during difficult times. Elaine will always be rememebered for her compassionate demeanor.

Passions & Interests

Elaine loved spending time in nature, whether it was relaxing at the beach with friends, studying horticulture in the wild, sketching flowers and landscapes, getting her hands dirty in the garden, or going for a run on a sunny day. She always said that nature was her muse and inspiration in her artwork.

Elaine always strived to raise awareness on chronic disease prevention and reversal through lifestyle within her local community. She was passionate about helping others reach their highest potential on a mental, physical, and spiritual level.
